Hi, my name is Matthew Turner, and this is my website! It is a work in progress, so bear with me. For right now this site is a bit of a graveyard of old links and some experimental stuff. In the future it may become a web resume or something similar. It could also become a personal blog of sorts. The truth is I don't have much in the way of plans for this website. I would like to keep it simple, fast-loading, and easily printable. I think that too much of the modern web has become bloated with websites that take a long time to load, are slow to use, and hide useful information away behind fancy menus and complex navigation.

If you like what you see or want to know more about me, my contact information is in the business card section on the top of this page.
